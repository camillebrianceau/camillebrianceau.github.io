About us

The Aramis Lab is powered by a diverse and talented team working together to advance brain health research. Our faculty lead the way with their expertise and vision, guiding the lab’s innovative projects. Post-doctoral researchers push the boundaries of knowledge, while our engineers build the cutting-edge tools that make it all possible. PhD students bring fresh ideas and energy, tackling some of the toughest challenges in neuroscience. Finally, our associate fellows enrich the lab with their specialized expertise and unique perspectives. Together, we’re dedicated to understanding brain diseases and transforming patient care.
