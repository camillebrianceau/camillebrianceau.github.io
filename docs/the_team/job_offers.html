Join our team

If you are interested in joining the team (with respect to any of our research topics), do not hesitate to send us an e-mail stating your field of interest and a CV. We may have other open positions than those advertised below. You can directly contact the head of the team for a general inquiry. Our team is pluridisciplinary and we welcome applicants with different profiles and expertises (computer science, electrical engineering, neuroimaging, neuroscience, medicine...).
